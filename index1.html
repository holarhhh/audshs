OUR MISSION STATEMENT

To educate all students to the highest levels of academic achievement, to enable them to reach and expand their potential, and to prepare them to become productive, responsible, ethical, creative and compassionate members of society

OUR HISTORY

The school was founded in 1961 by the Ansar-ud-deen society of Nigeria as a college for training in all aspects of commercial skills with emphasis on secretarial studies. The School was split into Junior and senior schools in Jan.2003. The school has a very strong old student association.
